📖 How to Play

  1. Pick your background. Your origin story determines your starting stats and quirks.
  2. Each day, you get Energy points (starts at 3). Spend them on feed cards — applying to jobs, posting on Linkfluence, networking, or resting.
  3. Apply to jobs to create leads. Leads progress through stages: Applied → Auto-Screen → Recruiter → Interview → Offer. But many will ghost you. Investigate before applying!
  4. Watch your stats. Rent drops every 5 days. If Hope hits 0, you spiral. If Burnout hits 100, you crash. If Money hits 0... well.
  5. Use Investigate cards. They cost Energy but reveal ghost jobs and scams, saving you from wasting it on dead ends.
  6. Survive 30 days. Land an offer, expose enough ghost jobs, or find another path to victory. Good luck. You'll need it.

Tip: Use Tab to navigate and Enter to activate buttons. Escape closes modals.
